About Us


Overview

Abington Heights Software Club is an Abington Heights organization dedicated to introducing and developing the computer skills necessary to succeed in the 21st century. We believe that computer skills are necessary for any passion, issue, or academic field, so we accept any student who is willing and motivated to learn and work in a team with people of different interests and levels of expertise. Our goal is to create a fun and supportive network of dedicated students that help each other pursue the fields of computer science, computer engineering, digital design, online management, and video game theory and discover how these fields interconnect with their own personal interests.
