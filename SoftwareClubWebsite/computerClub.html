Computer Club


About Us

The Computer club is a subsidiary of the Software Club. Just like our parent organization, we are based out of Abington Heights High School. Our goal is to collect unused, old, or retired computers and convert them into a usable product which we then donate to people/organizations who otherwise wouldn't be able to acquire them.



Drop-Off Locations

  • In-person students: Bring donations to Dr. William's room (510) and say it is for Computer Club.
  • Virtual students/General Public: Email ahsoftwareclub@gmail.com to organize a contactless pick-up from your house or a safe location.
  • More drop-off locations coming soon!

Drop-Off Instructions

  • If you are dropping off a computer, please limit handling of computer parts to limit the possibility of COVID-19 spread.
  • If our team is picking up a donation, we will not enter your home to pick up donations and we ask that you please respect health guidelines if you are interacting with our members. Please have all donations outside or in an exterior section of your home for our member to pick-up.

Desired Donations

Though we graciously accept all donations, we are in dire need for the following computer donations:

  • Hard drives
  • Graphics Cards
  • Monitors
  • Fully/Partially Built Computers
